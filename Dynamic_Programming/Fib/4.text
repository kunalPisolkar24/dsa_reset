Problem: Min Cost Climbing Stairs

You are given an integer array cost where:

cost[i] is the cost of stepping on stair i.

Once you pay the cost,
you can climb either:
- 1 step
- 2 steps

You may start from step 0 or step 1.

Return the minimum cost to reach the top.

--------------------------------------------------

Input:
An integer N
An array cost of size N

Output:
Print the minimum cost to reach the top.

--------------------------------------------------

Constraints:

2 <= N <= 1000
0 <= cost[i] <= 999

--------------------------------------------------

Example 1

Input:
3
10 15 20

Output:
15

Explanation:
Start at step 1 -> pay 15 -> jump to top.

--------------------------------------------------

Example 2

Input:
10
1 100 1 1 1 100 1 1 100 1

Output:
6

--------------------------------------------------

Example 3

Input:
2
5 10

Output:
5

--------------------------------------------------

Example 4

Input:
5
1 2 3 4 5

Output:
6
